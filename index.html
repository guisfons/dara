Dara is a group of hotels built with modernity, comfort, and atmosphere in mind.

Situated in Sihanoukville

Independence
Hotel

Independence Hotel Resort & Spa by Dara is the ultimate private beach resort in Cambodia, right on the leafy shores of Sihanoukville Bay. The hotel consists of historical buildings and seaside villas among acres of tropical vegetation and lush gardens. We offer our guests three restaurants, a swimming-pool complex, a private beach with jetty, as well as an ocean promenade. 155 well-located rooms on 22 acres of rainforest and tropical gardens await you, perfect for enjoying the ocean and a warm breeze.

Situated in Sihanoukville

Airport City
Hotel

Dara Airport City Hotel & Spa is located in Cambodia’s capital city, Phnom Penh. It is just a stone’s throw away from Phnom Penh International Airport and offers 226 stylish and comfortable rooms, including three suites and eleven apartments that are perfect for both business and leisure travelers looking for a cozy and memorable experience while staying in Phnom Penh.

visit hotel website

Situated in Sihanoukville

Olympia City
Hotel

Olympia City Hotel by Dara offers spacious suites designed, furnished, and decorated with modern comfort in mind, located in the urban heart of Phnom Penh in Cambodia, providing you with an exclusive view on the Olympic Stadium. Our hotel is also right close to the modern Olympia Shopping Mall. We provide you with deluxe studios, junior suites, as well as executive suites with high-speed internet and Wi-Fi access throughout the entire hotel and its facilities.
