Hotels

We are proud to say we count some of the country’s best hotels among the Dara Hotels group! Whether you’re looking to relax in a beautiful beachside resort in Sihanoukville Bay or you’d like to explore the buzzing urban center of Phnom Penh — we have a hotel wherever you’re going. We offer you stylish, modern, and comfortable hotels, paired with relaxing facilities like pools, bars, and spas, to make sure that you’re having the best vacation you ever had! Explore all our different hotels below; we’re sure you’ll find the perfect one just for you.

Our properties in Phnom Penh offer a deluxe experience with stylish hotels for business and leisure guests that are looking for a comfortable stay in the capital city of Cambodia.

Be in the urban heart of Phnom Penh, near the Olympic stadium and marvelous malls or conveniently near the international airport of Phnom Penh, all types of guests can enjoy a wonderful experience in comfort, cuisine and drinks.

DARA AIRPORT HOTEL & Spa

Dara Airport City Hotel & Spa offers 226 stylish rooms, including three suites and eleven apartments perfect for both business and leisure travelers looking for a comfortable and memorable experience while staying in Phnom Penh. The hotel’s proximity to the capital’s International Airport makes it the perfect place for your bleisure endeavors.

OLYMPIA CITY HOTEL

Our spacious suites were designed, furnished and decorated with modern comfort in mind. The hotel is located in the urban heart of Phnom Penh and provides you with an exclusive view on the Olympic Stadium and easy access to the modern Olympia Shopping Mall. Deluxe studios as well as junior and executive suites with high-speed internet access make your stay as enjoyable and comfortable as possible.
