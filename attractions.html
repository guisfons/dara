Attractions

Staying at one of Dara’s hotels means you’ll be able to explore everything we and Cambodia itself have to offer you! You can find many different activities inside our hotels but also around us, many of which are for both young and old. Whether you’re at one of our hotels on business or for leisure, you’ll find the perfect activity to take your mind off things right here.

We have properties in Sihanoukville Bay as well as Phnom Penh - both are destinations that could not be more thrilling and different from another. Are you looking for a relaxed vacation by the beach? Then why not stay at Independence Hotel? Are you on a business trip and need quick access to everything Phnom Penh has to offer? Then Dara Airport City Hotel & Spa or Olympia City Hotel might be just the right choice for you.

Just choose the hotel you want and get exploring!
