Dara Hotels’ Handling
of Guest Safety in
Light of COVID-19

At Dara Hotels and Jouvence Spa & Fitness, our number one priority is the well-being, health and safety of our guests, members, and team members.​ We continue to adhere to well-established procedures, plans and routines.​​ All of our hotels, serviced apartments, spas, and fitness centers are open and operate as normal, and our guests and members can be assured of their safety when staying at or visiting our hotels or any property under our management.

We follow now widely
well-established and
proven guidelines.

01.

All staff at Dara Hotels and Jouvence Spa & Fitness properties are well-versed with information about COVID-19 and best-practice safety precautions.

02.

Increased focus on hand hygiene and temperature taking at all of our properties.

03.

Strengthened cleaning and hygiene protocols.

04.

Regular disinfection of exposed surfaces such as door handles, card terminals, and lift buttons with anti-bacterial liquids.

05.

Offer disinfectant dispensers in public areas to our guests and members.

06.

Observance of the procedures for dealing with colleagues who are ill.

Security precautions that anyone can take to protect themselves and others from COVID-19:

  • Wear protective facemask
  • Frequent hand washing.
  • Have hand disinfection ready.
  • Avoid unnecessary hand contact.
  • Avoid touching eyes, mouth and nose.
  • Cover the mouth with the elbow when coughing and sneezing.
  • Keep at least two meters away from coughing or sneezing persons.
