Restaurants

A diverse and special set of meals and drinks are available to our guests at any of our hotels. Guests can choose to eat and drink in our elaborate and modern restaurants or bars, each with a different decoration and meticulously planned menus to provide you with the best gourmet experience in Cambodia.

This architectural restaurant offers you an unforgettable experience by opening up like a fan to the amazing ocean view of Sihanoukville Bay. Indoor and outdoor seating is available, as well as terraces, decks, and a VIP room.
